Better quality of life through an individually furnished home

zuppasoup.id May 24, 2024 INTERIOR DESIGN

A home where you feel comfortable and enjoy being is an essential prerequisite for a high quality of life and the most intense happiness possible.

Different aspects such as lighting, wall design, decoration and the position of furniture affect the quality of living. More and more people are choosing to decorate their homes according to the rules of Feng Shui in order to benefit from positive energies.

I will explain to you which aspects need to be taken into account when furnishing your home in this way.

Happiness depends on two factors: you and your good indoor climate!

Rely on high-quality lighting

Lighting is very important in a home that has been decorated according to Feng Shui rules. Care should be taken to create light-filled rooms to avoid a feeling of being cramped.

It is particularly advisable to rely on natural light. Large windows are useful and the light can be individually controlled using curtains and blinds. In particular, the desk and places where you spend a lot of time, such as the Feng Shui living room, should receive a lot of natural light.

Alternatively, you can use different light sources to illuminate different living areas. It is advisable not to use just one large ceiling light, especially in the Feng Shui bedroom.

This does not meet the individual requirements for tailor-made lighting . Instead, you should always use floor lamps and wall lamps to illuminate a reading corner, for example, and create an ambience that makes you feel comfortable.

Decorate your walls creatively

There are many ways to influence the atmosphere and design of a room through the walls.

For example, many people use Beton Cire to give the plaster an attractive appearance and to use a high-quality and attractive finish . The nice thing about this type of wall design is that it is easy to maintain and moisture-resistant.

In addition, different wall colors can be used to give rooms a special look and to separate different living areas from each other.

It is also a good idea to work with attractive wallpapers in different patterns and with creative designs. This way, a room quickly gets an individual touch and can be adapted to your style and taste.

Another good strategy is to work with attractive moldings. A variety of wall designs can be used throughout the entire apartment, or you can decide to give each room its own look.

The right decoration

The design of an apartment according to the Feng Shui principle depends not only on the walls and a high-quality floor covering , but also the decorative elements play an important role.

These are crucial to the personality of a room and whether it looks modern, traditional, youthful, stylish or futuristic. There are many different decorative items that you can use to design a room.

Among the most popular decorative elements are wall pictures. Here you should choose ones that show positive and nature-related motifs.

Likewise, many people work with attractive figures that are placed on a sideboard or window sill. Another strategy is to work with mirrors.

These can make a room appear larger and give it a particularly bright and appealing touch. However, you should avoid hanging a mirror opposite a door and mirrors are not welcome in a Feng Shui bedroom.

Otherwise it will reflect the positive energy that comes into the room, meaning it cannot be used.

Choose round shapes

Nature is characterized by round shapes. From the largest planets, which are round and orbit the sun in round orbits, to atoms, which are round and also move in round orbits, all elements in the world are basically round.

Round shapes are part of human nature and should play a major role in our lives. That is why it is advisable to use round shapes and to avoid edges and corners in furniture as much as possible.

In addition, by building houses, humans have, to a certain extent, distanced themselves from their original living environment.

That is why, according to the Feng Shui principle, it is important to bring as many natural materials as possible into your living space. This makes it possible to stay in touch with your roots and origins, even in big cities and modern environments, and to achieve the highest possible quality of life in your own home.

There is an old Chinese saying: even if your house has a thousand rooms, you only need one to sleep in.

 

Working with plants

To ensure a high quality of life in your own home, it is advisable to have as many plants as possible.

These bring liveliness and naturalness into your living area and noticeably improve the air quality.

Plants are particularly useful in Feng Shui bedrooms when it comes to improving the room climate and helping you to fall asleep and sleep through the night. Plants also bring a variety of colors into your living area, making it bright and cheerful. 

When selecting plants, their individual characteristics must be taken into account so that they fit perfectly into your living area and personal lifestyle.

Red wood, for example, represents fire in plants. If you want to make your living space warm, passionate and full of joy , you should choose this color. Dark leaves, on the other hand, represent water and bring depth and mobility to the living area.

Pastel-colored flowers, on the other hand, represent metal, which stands for courage and concentration. The element earth is represented by yellow and earth-colored plants and brings stability into your own four walls.

Appeal to all senses as much as possible

Humans have five senses that allow them to perceive their surroundings and improve their quality of life. However, most people often focus too much on their sense of sight, so that the other senses do not reach their full potential.

When furnishing your own living area, you should therefore try to appeal to as many senses as possible .

This can be achieved, for example, through water elements, wind chimes and quiet music. This stimulates the sense of hearing and calms the body and soul .

It is also advisable to work with different room scents. These can provide serenity and relaxation, but can also have an invigorating effect. It is a good idea to have different scents available for different phases of life and different moods.

Conclusion

By designing your own living space, it is easy to influence your personal quality of life and well-being. It is a good idea to follow Feng Shui rules when designing your own four walls.

Particular consideration should be given to lighting, walls, decoration, shapes and plants.
