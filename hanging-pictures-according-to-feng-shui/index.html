Hanging pictures according to Feng Shui

zuppasoup.id June 2, 2024 MURALS

Pictures and collages give a room a personal touch. And they create a very specific atmosphere. A picture says more than words and can evoke different emotions. It can liven up a room or create a calm mood. Pictures can therefore be used excellently in the spirit of Feng Shui to bring Qi into your home. We explain what you should consider when placing pictures in the spirit of Feng Shui .

When designing walls in accordance with Feng Shui, it is not only important how much and what is hung, but also where the pictures are placed.

Feng Shui is known to be applicable to a wide range of areas, from the location and architecture of the house to decoration and wall art. In fact, in terms of Feng Shui, no one is doing themselves any favors by having empty walls. On the other hand, it is not good for the Qi if too much is hanging on the walls. This could lead to a disruption of the energy flow and cause fatigue more quickly.

The selection of images

It all starts with the selection of images. Before you have your photos and posters printed or buy paintings and drawings, you should think carefully about which motifs have a positive effect on you. For example, if you love old family photos, you should look within yourself and ask yourself whether the old black and white photos with the serious-looking people evoke the mood in you that you want in your home.

It is also important that a motif fits the Feng Shui room . The overall picture must appear harmonious and coherent. It is helpful if a picture or a collage reflects the basic idea of ​​a room. A picture of a pond fits in the bathroom, and photos of the family fit in the living room.

According to the principles of Feng Shui, it is usually better to work with several small pictures arranged as a harmonious collage, rather than with a single large picture, which can easily appear “overwhelming”.

Classic Feng Shui images  show bamboo, koi fish, the Chinese dragon or Buddha, for example. These images can be used to attract wealth and prosperity. In general, natural motifs are extremely popular in Feng Shui.

Orientation according to the cardinal directions

When hanging the pictures, you can use the cardinal directions as a guide. This is because the cardinal directions are aligned with certain energies that the corresponding elements can make use of.

The element water

In Feng Shui, the element of water in a room is associated with career and success. According to Feng Shui principles, pictures with water elements are recommended for hanging on a north wall in order to use the positive energies for success. Wall pictures with rivers, waterfalls or sea motifs are good for strengthening the element of water in a room. This can be a painting or a photo from your last beach holiday that you can have printed as a poster .

The element fire

The fire element stands for rank, fame and prestige. The south wall is therefore associated with the element of fire. It is supposed to bring together positive energies for this area of ​​life. Where there is fire, there is usually a need for wood. This is also suitable for south walls. Pictures of trees and forests are therefore suitable. A little "trick" can be to frame a picture in a wooden frame. Water motifs should generally be avoided, because if the fire element is to be strengthened, the water element is counterproductive.

The element wood

In Feng Shui, the wood element corresponds to health and family and has the greatest effect in the east. An east wall should therefore not be left empty. A collection of pictures that promote vitality and awaken positive energies are perfect for the east wall. Anything that feels good is allowed. These can be photos of nature or pictures of loved ones.

The element metal

Metal represents clarity and is strongest in the West. It is strengthened by the colors gold, silver and white. Harmony is important here, but bolder metal artwork and round ornaments in a metallic frame can provide structure.

Tip: The choice of frame for a picture should not be neglected in terms of Feng Shui. It should harmonize with the painting, photo or poster print in terms of color and shape and create a good overall picture.

The wall facing the bed

It is the first thing you see when you wake up and the last thing you see in the evening before you go to sleep: the wall facing the bed. In the spirit of Feng Shui , an inspiring wall picture is particularly important in the bedroom . What inspires you is something you can and should interpret very personally.

Pictures of symbols or people that give you confidence are particularly suitable. A motivational quote is also conceivable. The colors of the wall facing the bed should be calming. 

Beige, peach, light blue and green tones and also a soft brown are suitable. You should especially avoid pictures that appear gloomy and can cause fear and sadness. According to the principles of Feng Shui, a mirror is also considered a bad choice for the wall opposite the bed.

 

The wall in the office

What you see is what you get. According to the principles of Feng Shui, the wall in your Feng Shui office should bring positive energy and not block you. We look at this wall so often over a large part of the day. And at a time of the day when we need inspiration and motivation. 

Therefore, the same approach applies as for the bedroom wall. It is also conceivable to have a vision board created according to the principles of Feng Shui, on which goals, wishes and dreams are lovingly recorded using beautiful motifs and symbols.
