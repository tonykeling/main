The living trends 2024 – Inspirations for Feng Shui furnishing

zuppasoup.id May 27, 2024 FURNISHING

The year 2024 has already started off stressfully with the many crises. Since you will certainly be spending a lot of time at home, it is important that you really feel comfortable.

You design the Feng Shui living room according to your own preferences as your refuge to feel safe. You can  find inspiration in the current living trends for 2024. You can find out what is currently popular and how you can design your own home as harmoniously as possible here.

Art Deco replaces Bauhaus in 2024 home trends

 

While the Bauhaus style conquered the houses in 2019, Art Deco is currently making its mark this year . Furniture and accessories have metal feet , while playful patterns on lush textiles add an elegant touch. 

The elegant design shows itself at its best in combination with rich, dark colors such as blue or green. You can complement the elegance of the furnishing style with beautiful textiles or other decorative elements. Velvet cushions and patterned wallpaper are elegant without appearing kitschy in the  2024 home trends.

 

The motto here is to create a pompous and opulent environment. To ensure that your Feng Shui living room  doesn't appear chaotic, don't mix too many colors , patterns and materials, but rather focus on a few favorites. 

Therefore, place beautiful eye-catchers sparingly to avoid a boastful appearance, while at the same time highlighting the individual pieces in a positive way.

Natural accents prevail in the living trends of 2024

 

This year, furniture with wickerwork in various forms is enjoying great popularity. These include, for example:

  • Chairs
  • Bistro tables
  • Sideboards
  • Lamps
 

The Viennese wickerwork design has existed for almost 200 years and is now making a comeback in the interior design scene. The wickerwork made of rattan is supported by modern accessories made of bamboo, raffia or seagrass and is particularly popular in Feng Shui kitchens .

The so-called boho look can therefore be perfectly combined with it, as it is based on precisely these natural materials. In addition, Japanese-style furniture looks good with Viennese wickerwork.

Especially in the Feng Shui bedroom and Feng Shui living room, wickerwork elements create an exotic look that stands out even more with light and earthy tones. However, you should avoid too many intense colors as they cause unrest.

 

Nordic design continues to evolve

 

The Scandinavian interior design style, known in recent years for its minimalist furniture and simple colors, is brightening up this year with a little more color . Instead of the muted white, beige or gray tones, light or white wood is now presented alongside colorful individual pieces . Pastel colors complement the simple design and create real eye-catchers with their contrasts - the Feng Shui office in particular benefits from this .

The Warm Nordic design not only ensures comfort and tranquility through the understated furniture, but also brings life into your home through the various colorful elements. You can set accents, for example, with:

  • A mustard yellow duvet
  • Colorful picture walls above the sofa
  • Pastel colored boxes in the kitchen
 

Further current in minimalism

 

Another trend based on simplicity is "Modern Living". Similar to Nordic design, simple designs and muted colors with a minimalism in mind dominate here . Warm and earthy tones such as beige, gray and brown are in the foreground, with colors such as blue, yellow, green and white occasionally complementing the overall picture.

The focus of the  2024 home trends  is primarily on the origin of the materials. This shows the desire for sustainability and high-quality pieces from environmentally friendly production.

 

The following materials are primarily used:

 
  • rattan
  • Stone
  • Linen
  • Wood
  • Basket
 

These provide peace and comfort, which is why the style is increasingly popular in living rooms and bedrooms .

Organic lamp bases

 

The return to natural materials can also be seen in the lighting . Lamp bases made of wood , glass and ceramic stand out in contrast to urban plastic or metal . 

Here you will find above all fine shapes and playful designs that stylishly round off the trend of elegant furnishings. Especially in an otherwise simple interior design philosophy, wooden lamps provide coziness and act as real eye-catchers.

 

The Glass Revival

 

Glass is not only a great choice for lamps. As a stand-alone decorative element, it doesn't look cold and minimalist this year, but rather boasts beautiful tones and polished structures .

  • Vases
  • Lamps
  • Candlesticks

With a combination of white, copper and gold, they give the entire interior a noble touch.

 

Living trends - Pastel conquers the walls

 

Last year, pastel colors were already an indispensable part of homes . The cheerful colors make a room immediately seem inviting. London Fashion Week crowned the colors "Fiery Red, Peach Pink, Empire Yellow and Love Bird" as the most important colors of the coming year. 

There is also the light green “Classic Green”, the color has a refreshing and cool effect and perfectly complements any pastel shade. On the wall, the color shines even more intensely if you complement it with darker wood or warm gold or copper accents . 

In addition, old pink or a matte yellow can be found on both walls and furniture. Especially in kitchens and dining rooms, the light colors in combination with gold decoration and furniture made of light wood are a real mood-booster .

The Pantone Color Institute, on the other hand, chose the color "Classic Blue" as the most distinctive. In addition to this, other tones such as gray-blue and aqua are becoming increasingly popular because they bring the vastness of the sea into your home and make the rooms appear larger . 

Thanks to the variety of shades of blue, you can find the right color for every room. Combined with terracotta and rich green tones or dark wooden furniture, the sea tones look impressive.

 

The circle closes

 

Away from the corners and edges of last year, round furniture is making its mark this year. The oval shapes and flowing edges create a retro look that doesn't stop at seating and upholstered furniture. They are also being taken into the Feng Shui children's room .

Round lamps and vases suit both modern interiors and older-style apartments . They provide peace and balance and look very classy when combined with dark wood and earth tones.

Bringing the look into your own home is not difficult. If possible, swap out square furniture for round furniture. In smaller steps, it already proves to be effective to swap out accessories and small pieces of furniture for new ones in organic shapes.
