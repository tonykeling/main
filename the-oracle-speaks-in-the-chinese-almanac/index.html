“The Oracle Speaks” in the Chinese Almanac

zuppasoup.id May 23, 2024 ORACLE

Every year there is a Chinese almanac. It contains a lot of information and describes what events the year will bring. It not only contains the "good days" for starting construction and moving in, but also a lot of traditional information about life.

Each day has different qualities and can be assigned to certain events.

The almanac is used by the Chinese population for all life events.

You can use this reference book for a wide range of topics, including celebrations, meetings with important business partners, or cleaning the house, especially the altar. A good almanac will describe each individual Chinese zodiac sign's benefits for the day.

In the West we use the lunar calendar.

The lunar calendar tells you a lot, like when is the right day to go to the hairdresser? Some people swear that it makes their hair grow faster or that their hairstyle looks better overall.
But there are also reports that amaze us. If the Christmas tree is felled according to the lunar calendar, it does not lose its needles so quickly.

The moon allows many seeds to yield larger harvests and plants to thrive better. The Chinese almanac also relies on the signs of the moon, but always in conjunction with other important stars in the sky.

 

Almanac and Feng Shui together support favorable opportunities.

Relying solely on the calendar would be a superficial view of events. There are many other techniques in  Feng Shui to calculate favorable days for Feng Shui measures and moving into a new house . However, this requires the exact birth date of the respective house occupant and the exact orientation of the entire house.

If you stick to the “favorable times,” not only will the move go smoothly, but the Feng Shui consultations will also be more effective and, above all, have an immediate positive effect.

To calculate these times, most classical Feng Shui consultants use the solar calendar.

This provides a time frame for renovation work for each house . In order to calculate the time frame for renovation precisely, precise measurements must be taken using the Feng Shui compass .

A funny detail of this calendar is information about various "oracle systems". It doesn't matter whether it's about blinking at certain times or sneezing at a certain time. Everything is precisely documented and traced back to certain possible events.

Oracles and the resonances.

It is not only the Chinese who have a series of predictions about certain events; we also have many customs that are mostly dismissed as superstition today. Despite all this, many people adhere to them and some oracles even come true.

After all, we live in an environment of resonances. Everything we radiate, do and behave in can be interpreted and will result in events. Have fun with my oracle about sneezing. I look forward to hearing about your experiences.

 

Today I want to give you a special gift and introduce you to this simple oracle.

From today on, pay special attention to when and at what time you sneeze.
Have you ever sneezed without having a cold? There's an old saying: "Sneezing once means someone is thinking of you. Sneezing twice in a row means someone will scold you.
“Sneezing three times indicates that someone is talking about you.” In traditional Chinese folklore, sneezing is interpreted as a kind of preview of events.
