Your home garden – your balcony becomes your oasis of well-being!

zuppasoup.id May 31, 2024 GARDEN DESIGN

A balcony can enrich the quality of life in any season. With the right design ideas, the storage area for water crates becomes an additional living space that conveys a holiday feeling and provides fresh energy.

Balcony planting: How to make your balcony your favourite place

A balcony can be an oasis of relaxation all year round. Healthy, thriving plants exude positive energy and provide strength. Combine evergreen and winter-hardy plants such as cypresses or flowering flowers that tolerate sun and heat. 

The choice of color depends on your preferences. It is ideal if there is always something blooming and rich, green colors provide lively energy.

Choose plants that are suitable for their location so that they feel comfortable on your balcony. Perennials and sun-loving flowers as well as shade-loving shrubs are grateful for a lovingly and appropriately selected spot. If you want to avoid stress with watering, you could also install automatic watering systems.

The balcony as a snack garden

Fresh herbs, vegetables and fruit are not just reserved for garden owners. Self-sufficiency is the trend. 

A snack garden is a real urban highlight and can be quickly set up on a balcony or a small terrace. From a Feng Shui perspective , healthy and natural food is full of vitality and helps to activate the Qi.   Various buckets in different colors and sizes are an ideal solution. 

If you want to make good use of the limited space on your balcony, you could later enjoy crispy vegetables from your own garden. It's great fun to watch plants and fruit grow - and home-grown fruit tastes incomparably good! 

Small fig and olive trees in attractive planters, which even bear fruit, create a Mediterranean flair and holiday feeling. Pleasantly fragrant Mediterranean herbs such as rosemary and thyme attract bees and other beneficial insects. 

If you like, you can always meet your need for fresh herbs by planting a bucket with your favorite herbs. Hanging plant baskets are also suitable for this. In Feng Shui, every room, including the balcony, should provide good energy and vitality, so you should treat every part of your living space with care.

Keeping order on the balcony

There is never enough space. Unfortunately, many balconies are so small that creativity is limited. 

Before you invest in the design of your balcony, you first need to tidy up. The smaller the balcony, the more important one thing is from a Feng Shui perspective : order . 

No matter how tempting it is to quickly remove the rubbish or deposit bottles from sight and put them on the balcony when it rains - it looks unsightly and blocks energy, which cannot circulate freely. 

A balcony looks tidier when plants and furniture are arranged in small groups.

How can I decorate my balcony?

Many balconies face south because of the sunshine. A south-facing balcony pampers the resident with warmth and brightness. 

Larger balconies offer space for seating sets where you can relax and enjoy warm summer evenings. But you can also find space for suitable furniture on a small balcony. 

According to the motto “less is more”, all places and corners of the balcony should be freely accessible. This is practical and allows the energy to circulate freely in Feng Shui . 

Sharp edges pose a risk of injury – balcony furniture with rounded corners, on the other hand, brings harmony to the balcony. A combination of different materials, colors or structures creates excitement. For example, strong and subtle colors can be combined.

Observe yourself. See how you are and feel inside yourself.
